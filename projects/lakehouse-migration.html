01 / Context

The problem.

A 7-year-old ETL stack on legacy infra was breaking weekly. Nightly batches ran 6 hours, frequently missing the 6 AM SLA for downstream reporting. Adding a new source meant a 3-week change-management cycle. The platform team was firefighting instead of delivering.

02 / Approach

How it works.

  • Medallion architecture: Bronze (raw) → Silver (cleansed) → Gold (mart) on Delta Lake.
  • Strangler pattern: Migrated pipelines in waves, running old & new in parallel for 2 weeks each with diff-checks.
  • Orchestration: Airflow DAGs replace cron-driven shell scripts; lineage captured automatically.
  • IaC: Terraform manages every workspace, cluster policy and job — no clickops.
NDA: Source system details, schema specifics and migration playbook are abstracted.
03 / Outcomes

What shipped.

  • Nightly batch dropped from 6 hours to 38 minutes.
  • Infrastructure spend reduced 62% through better cluster sizing and spot instances.
  • Time-to-onboard a new source: 3 weeks → 2 days.
  • Unlocked ad-hoc analytics workloads the old stack couldn't support.
