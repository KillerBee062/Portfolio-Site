P / 03 — Dashboards

Executive Pulse Dashboard.

A single-screen command centre that tracks margin, working capital and SLA across 14 markets — refreshed every 15 minutes so the C-suite never makes decisions on yesterday's data.

01 / Context

The problem.

The leadership team was getting six separate weekly decks — one per business unit — none of which agreed on the numbers. Margin definitions varied, SLA thresholds were inconsistent, and by the time the board pack was assembled it was already a week stale.

02 / Approach

How it works.

  • Semantic layer: A single Synapse-backed model defines margin, working capital and SLA once, used by every visual.
  • Refresh pipeline: Incremental refresh every 15 minutes; alerts fire if any feed misses SLA.
  • One screen: A single Power BI page that scales from board-room TV to phone, with drill-throughs on every tile.
  • Annotation layer: Each KPI has an owner + "what changed since last week" tooltip.
NDA: KPI definitions, market list and metric thresholds are abstracted.
03 / Outcomes

What shipped.

  • Replaced 6 weekly decks with one live dashboard.
  • Cut "what's the real number" arguments in leadership reviews.
  • Saved ~20 analyst-hours/week previously spent on deck assembly.
  • Now the default view in every weekly C-suite review.
